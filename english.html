English Class - Travel Writing Mind Map

Topic: Keelung Night Market

  • General Description: Unique, Old, Cool, Spirited, Lively, Loud, Crowded
  • When & Who: When was it built? Who built it?
  • Food Questions:
    • Is the food expensive?
    • How good is the quality?
    • How much food will I buy?
    • Which one is the best?
  • Food Variety: Colorful snacks, Pork, Duck
  • Souvenirs: Are there good souvenirs?
  • Money: How much money will I spend?
  • People: Are the sellers nice or rude?
